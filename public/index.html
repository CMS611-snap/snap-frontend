"Snap!" is a playful activity that helps groups to quickly understand and see what they think about a given topic

Digital Game created by Shalom Abate, Tej Chajed, Xinyue Deng, Sabrina Drammis, Sam Edson, Daniel Sanchez, Zygimantas Straznickas, Shuang Yin as part of a course by the MIT Game Lab in collaboration with the Red Cross/Red Crescent Climate Centre
